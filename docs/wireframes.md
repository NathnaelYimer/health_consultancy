# Low-fidelity Wireframes

## Homepage (vertical flow)
- Top nav: Logo | Home | About | Projects | Services | Publications | Blog | Contact | Language
- Hero: Headline, subheadline, CTAs [Request Consultation] [Explore Research]
- What We Do: 4 cards (Maternal health, Trials, Policy, Education)
- Latest Insights: 3 latest posts with thumbnail + Read
- Impact summary: quick stats
- Footer: contact, social, legal links

## Article / Publication page
- Title
- Author | Date | Download PDF
- Abstract / Summary
- Full content (in-page multimedia links)
- Related content column or block
- Cite this: preformatted citation

## Consultation flow (multi-step)
1. Step 1: Select service (consultation type)
2. Step 2: Provide contact info + short case summary
3. Step 3: Consent & payment (if paid) or scheduling
4. Confirmation page with ticket ID and expected response time

## Contact/Feedback
- Simple form with name, email, subject, message, consent checkbox, submit
